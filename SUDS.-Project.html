The background.

SUDS. is a website that is not based off of a real store. Even though it is a fake website, SUDS. deals with real issues within the e-commerce design platforms. I have modeled the design after a small business that has the potential for growth. The small business is a soap and bath bomb botique. I created the design to fix many of the issues that often arise for users when using e-Commerce designs.

Solving problems through design.

In a world that is constantly developing in the world of technology, consumers have turned to the online platform for commerce stores. “e-commerce” is the most popular form of shopping in the modern world. With the large growth in e-commerce stores, new challenges have come to light that cause UX designers to focus their attention on. In order to grasp the issues that users face when shopping e-commerce, I did extensive user research on the topic. Through examining the research I decided to focus on fixing three of the main issues I found. I then transformed these issues into design focuses or goals for the website. These include:

  • A clean design that is easy for users to navigate through.

  • A design that is completley transparent to thr user, and encourages a sense of security for them.
  • An efficient checkout experience that encourages the user to finalize their purchase.

Building trust with users.

It is vital that users feel a sense of security when purchasing from online e-commerce stores. One of the ways I improved this was by including reviews in every product. While many stores do include this, there is often a lack of personality and information provided to the user. Through the use of personal reviews, product statistics, and overall transparency, users will feel more secure throughout their shopping experience.
